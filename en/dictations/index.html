💡 Ear Training Concept

🔍 Dictation Aids Panel

If you have difficulty identifying the notes by ear, use these aids. Each reduces your final score.

📖 Aid 1: Detailed Melodic Hint

Describes the movement of intervals in the melody (stepping up, down, or repeating).

-5%

🎯 Aid 2: Highlight Silent Note on Staff

Highlights the correct pitches in red on the staff to see their relative height.

-10%

🟢 Aid 3: Reveal Correct Note in Palette

Highlight in green color in the bottom palette the next correct note you need to place.

-15%
